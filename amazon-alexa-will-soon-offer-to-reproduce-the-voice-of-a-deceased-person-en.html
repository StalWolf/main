Amazon's alexa voice assistant will soon
be able to mimic the voice of a deceased loved one.

Sometimes you think technology is going too far. Here, that may well be the case: Rohit Prasad, Amazon's chief scientist, announced last Wednesday that Amazon was working to make its alexa voice assistant capable of mimicking a person's voice from from an extract of only one minute. The ad doesn't stop there: in the video example given by amazon, a child can be seen asking him: ‚ÄúAlexa, can Grandma finish reading me The Wizard of Oz?‚Äù The device then changes its voice to that of the grandmother.
